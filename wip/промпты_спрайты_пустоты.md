# Промпты: твари Пустоты (EN, под тот же генератор, что Hero/учёные)

> Нужные листы (как у Hero): **walk** down/right/up и **attack** down/right/up,
> сетка 5×5, кадр 256×256, прозрачный фон. Палитра подобрана под разрыв в игре:
> тело #1a0f2e–#2e1550, свечение #8844cc, глаза/ядро #cc66ff–#ff66ff.

## Void Stalker (сталкер — быстрый охотник)

```
A lean predatory void creature for a 2D top-down RPG: a hunched four-limbed
stalker made of semi-transparent dark purple smoke and shadow (#1a0f2e body,
#3a1d66 wisps), its edges constantly fraying into mist. Two bright glowing
violet eyes (#cc66ff) with no other facial features. Long claw-like forelimbs.

should look unfinished, dissolving. Creepy, fast, alien — not demonic, not

laboratory scene. Sprite sheet, 5x5 grid, 256x256 px per frame, transparent

```

## Void Colossus (Колосс — босс второй волны)

```
A massive void colossus boss for a 2D top-down RPG: a towering bulky giant of
layered black-violet shadow plates (#0e0620, #33156b) over a blinding
magenta-white energy core in its chest (#ff66ff core, white center) that
pulses through the cracks. Huge asymmetric arms — one ends in a hammer-like
mass of compressed darkness. Fragments of broken reality (small glowing
shards) orbit its shoulders. Edges dissolve into heavy smoke. Slow,
unstoppable, terrifying — a siege engine made of nothing. Half-material:
silhouette partially translucent at the limbs. Chibi-heavy proportions
consistent with a steampunk laboratory scene, reads clearly at small size.
Sprite sheet, 5x5 grid, 256x256 px per frame, transparent background, no
text. Animations: slow heavy walk (ground-shaking stomp) and attack
(overhead smash with the hammer-arm, core flaring bright).
```

## Бонус, если генератор потянет (необязательно)

- **death/dissolve** (один лист на каждого): тварь схлопывается внутрь и
  рассеивается в дым — тел они не оставляют, идеально под лор.
- **Машина Переноса** (статичная, побольше): brass-and-copper steampunk
  transfer machine, vertical glass capsule with swirling blue ether light
  (#66ccff) inside, Tesla coils and pipes, riveted panels, 512x512, front view,
  transparent background.

## Как отдать в игру

Файлы в `so/converted_images/` (как Hero) → пуш → я разложу в
`public/assets/lab/` и подключу: `void_stalker_*` / `void_colossus_*`
анимации подхватятся Creature автоматически.
