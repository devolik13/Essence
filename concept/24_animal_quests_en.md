# Animal Quests — Chapter 1

A quest appears automatically when the Sphere inhabits an animal body.
Completing it is optional. But each quest is the fastest way to earn 50 XP
and unlock the body's signature skill.

The quest lives within the animal's own logic. The Sphere doesn't set a goal —
it feels what this body wants or needs to do.

---

## Rabbit `rabbit`
**Skill:** ⚡ Acceleration — +50% speed for 3 sec

### Quest: "The Smell of Danger"

*[Text on possession]*
> *The world is different. The grass is enormous. Every sound is a threat.*
> *Somewhere high above, a shadow circles. A hawk. The burrow is far.*
> *The body knows what to do. Run.*

**Objective:** Get from the field to the burrow without being caught by the hawk.
The hawk patrols from above — it dives when it spots movement in the open.
Use bushes, shadows, pauses. And at the right moment — sprint.

**Mechanic:** First real-pressure training for Acceleration.
The rabbit cannot fight. Only speed and cover.

**Ending:** The burrow. Darkness. Warmth. Safe.
*50 XP earned. Acceleration unlocked.*

**World connection:** This same rabbit sometimes appears on Farmer Ed's garden —
he complains about the damage. If the player completed the rabbit quest,
Ed has a line: *"That ginger one got through again. Fast as lightning."*

## Wolf `wolf`
**Skill:** ⚔ Sword Strike — melee, base 18, 20% slow

### Quest: "A Hungry Pack"

*[Text on possession]*
> *Smells speak louder than words. The pack is hungry — two days without prey.*
> *The alpha watches. Waiting. The young one has to prove itself.*
> *A deer was here an hour ago. The trail leads east.*

**Objective:** Track the deer by scent and bring it down.
The deer is faster in a straight line — cut off escape routes,
drive it toward the cliff, work by instinct.
The final lunge — a precise strike at the right moment.

**Mechanic:** Introduction to tracking (new mechanic in wolf body),
pursuit tactics. First use of Sword Strike as a hunting lunge.

**Ending:** Prey taken. The pack is fed. The alpha nods.
*50 XP earned. Sword Strike unlocked.*

**World connection:** This wolf is the same one the Sphere will encounter
in the Grey Ravine during main Quest 3.
If the quest was completed — the wolf recognizes the Sphere. Trusts it immediately.
If not — it takes time for it to allow approach.

## Bear `bear`
**Skill:** 🔨 Crushing Blow — melee, base 22, 100% concentration break

### Quest: "Strangers in the Grove"

*[Text on possession]*
> *This forest is mine. Always has been.*
> *But today it smells wrong. A campfire. Metal. People.*
> *Too close to the den.*

**Objective:** Drive off a bandit camp at the western forest edge.
Not kill — frighten. The bear doesn't want blood without cause.
Three by the fire — appear suddenly, roar, overturn, strike flat.
They must run.

**Mechanic:** Introduction to Crushing Blow as a control tool,
not just damage. Concentration break — bandits can't aim,
drop torches, lose their footing.

**Ending:** The camp abandoned. The forest quiet again.
The bear returns to the den. Lies down.
*50 XP earned. Crushing Blow unlocked.*

**World connection:** The bandits driven off by the bear
are the same camp in the Stone Hills. If the quest was completed,
the camp will be short-handed with a line: *"A bear wrecked everything.
Three of them still haven't come back."*
