## The Four Guardians — Chapter 1 Bosses

Each Guardian protects its place. They are not evil —
they simply don't allow outsiders on their territory.
Defeat a Guardian = prove strength = receive its frequency.

---

### Ignis — Guardian of Fire
**Location:** Center of the Ash Grove, old volcanic crater

**Appearance:** A massive humanoid figure of molten lava.
Three times the height of a person. Eyes — two craters.

**Combat:**
- Leaves fire trails when walking
- Phase 1: slow, powerful strikes
- Phase 2 (50% HP): splits into 3 mini-Spark bosses, then reforms —
  vulnerable while split
- Weakness: Water

**Reward:** spell **Fireball** — damage + ignite for 3 sec
*+ First frequency of the Seal of Elements*

---

### Aquaris — Guardian of Water
**Location:** Island in the center of the Misty Lake

**Appearance:** A wave-like figure of water, constantly changing shape.

**Combat:**
- Arena — small island, water all around (fall = damage + reset)
- Periodically dives — invulnerable, resurfaces on the other side
- Slows player with water projectiles
- Weakness: Earth, Fire

**Reward:** spell **Water Shield** — absorbs 2 hits,
reflects part of damage back
*+ Second frequency of the Seal of Elements*

---

### Terra — Guardian of Earth
**Location:** Main hall of the Abandoned Mine, Stone Hills

**Appearance:** A massive stone golem. Slow. Every step shakes the ground.

**Combat:**
- Highest physical damage of the four
- Goes underground — surfaces beneath the player's feet
- Summons Pebbles (2–3) for help
- Weakness: Wind, Water

**Reward:** spell **Stone Fist** — strike + stun for 1.5 sec
*+ Third frequency of the Seal of Elements*

---

### Aeros — Guardian of Wind
**Location:** Summit of the main cliff, Wind Peaks

**Appearance:** Nearly invisible — a silhouette of swirling air,
glowing eyes. The fastest of the four.

**Combat:**
- Constantly moving
- Blows player off the cliff edge (instant death beyond the edge)
- Creates 3 clones — 1 real among 3 phantoms
- Weakness: Earth, Fire

**Reward:** spell **Wind Gust** — pushes enemy back, interrupts attack
*+ Fourth frequency → Seal of Elements complete*

## Seal of Elements

Assembled from four frequencies — one from each Guardian.
This is not an object. It is a pattern the Sphere carries within itself.

**Use:** seals warp rifts in the Steam World.
Used once at the end of Chapter 1.
In subsequent chapters — strengthened by new frequencies.

**Why it works:** the Guardians' frequencies are the resonance of this world.
Warp rifts operate on the same foundation as the Void.
The pattern of four elements creates a "seal" — closes a rift
stably, not temporarily as before.

## What the Player Gets at the End of Chapter 1

**Skills (from Guardians):**
- Fireball — damage + ignite
- Water Shield — defense + reflect
- Stone Fist — damage + stun
- Wind Gust — control + push

**Seal of Elements** — ability to close warp rifts.

**Mechanics mastered:** body switching, first animal (wolf),
understanding that different bodies serve different purposes.

**Lore:**
- Valdmar — something is happening between the cities
- Nameless Stranger — who is he? Why did he know about the Guardians?

**The feeling:**
The world is alive. It doesn't know your truth.
You were here — and you left. And they remained.
