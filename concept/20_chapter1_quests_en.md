## Awakening in a Foreign Body

*[Text on first entry into Ashworth — same for all three starter bodies]*

A village. The smell of smoke, livestock, fresh bread.
Unfamiliar faces — but the body knows them. Vaguely.
Like a dream almost recalled.

Someone nods as they see you. You nod back.
You don't know this person. The body does.

The Sphere looks around. Four directions — four pulls.
Something draws toward all four at once.

## Quest 1 — Trial by Fire *(Guardian: Ignis)*

### Trigger

**Hunter Bert**, simply and to the point:
*"Heard the legend of the Guardians? Fire, water, earth, wind.
Four places around us. They say whoever defeats all four receives their gift.
I don't believe in such things. But you — looks like you're heading somewhere anyway."*

This isn't a quest. It's a conversation. But it opens the map.

**Blacksmith Aldric**, without looking up:
*"You said you'd come back with the stone from the grove. Where is it?"*

The Sphere doesn't remember this conversation.

*[Player can ask]:*
*"I forgot — which stone exactly?"*

Aldric sets down his hammer. Looks up.
*"Volcanic. With red veins. From the crater in the Ash Grove.
You volunteered yourself. Said you weren't afraid.
Changed your mind?"*

### In the Ash Grove

A burned forest. The ground is hot underfoot.
Swarms of Sparks dart between charred trunks.
Ashers trudge slowly — after death they leave burning patches.

At the center of the grove — an old volcanic crater. **Ignis** is there.

He doesn't attack. He stands. He watches.
The Guardian only accepts a challenge from someone who approaches first.

*[Player initiates combat]*

### After Victory

Ignis dissolves. The ground cools beneath the feet.
In the air — a pulsing warmth. The Sphere feels it enter and stay.
*First frequency accepted.*

Among the hot stones near the crater — the stone with red veins.

Aldric takes the stone. Long silence.
*"You made it then."*
Nothing more. But he looks differently now.

## Quest 2 — The Price of Fog *(Guardian: Aquaris)*

### Trigger

**Mira**, morning, with worry:
*"Father hasn't gotten up for three days. Fever.
Old Helga says Misty Lotus will help —
grows only on the island in the lake.
You were planning to go there before, right?
Remember where the crossing is?"*

*[If the player asks about the crossing]:*
*"Come on, you forgot? Bert showed you — by the old willow, the north trail.
You even said the fog there was strange."*

### At the Misty Lake

The fog is thick — the bank disappears ten steps in.
Splashers leap from the water, slowing on hit.
Foggers glide between the trees, draining mana.

An island in the middle of the lake. Lotus flowers at the water's edge.
**Aquaris** rises from the depths when the Sphere steps onto the bank.
A wave-like figure, constantly changing shape.
The arena — a small island. Water all around. Fall in — damage and return.

### After Victory

The fog over the lake thins — for the first time in years.
*Second frequency accepted.*

The lotus is delivered. By evening Aldric is recovering.
Mira: *"Thank you. Really."*
Then quieter, almost to herself:
*"You've changed somehow. I don't know. Never mind."*

## Quest 3 — What the Hills Hide *(Guardian: Terra + dungeon)*

### Trigger

**Bert**, concerned:
*"Miner Sven went into the shaft two days ago. Hasn't come back.
You know the way there — we went together three weeks ago.
Remember the entrance?"*

*[If you ask]:*
*"Through the Grey Ravine, where the cliff has a crack in it.
You even said the echo there was strange."*

### The Grey Ravine — First Body Switch

On the way to the mine — **the Grey Ravine**. Wolf territory.
A few wolves near the trail. One looks directly at you.

*[Hint appears for the first time]:*
*You feel it — you can enter. This wolf is calm. It won't run.*

*[First animal possession — tutorial moment]*

The world changes instantly.
Smells — enormous, volumetric, saying more than eyes.
The ground is closer. The gait is different. The body wants to run.

The wolf knows the path to the mine better than any human — by scent.
It can smell Sven. Sven was here recently. The trail leads down.

*[Player travels part of the way in the wolf's body]*

At the mine entrance — a narrow gap in the rock. A human won't fit.
The wolf slips through easily.

### The Abandoned Mine

Darkness. But the wolf perceives differently — movement, heat, scent.
Pebbles in narrow corridors. Mudders near the underground stream.
Several rooms. Cave-ins block old passages.

In the far hall — **Sven**. Alive. Sitting against the wall, holding a book.

He sees the wolf. Doesn't panic — just looks strangely.
*"You... you're not an ordinary wolf. Your eyes are different."*

Sven talks about the mage's diary. About the Guardians. About frequencies.
Then: *"Terra is at the exit. A wolf can't beat him."*

*[Player leaves the wolf — returns to their starter body]*

The body was waiting outside. Slightly dazed. Alive.

### Battle with Terra

**Terra** blocks the exit from the main hall. Every step shakes the tunnel.
Slow. Goes underground — surfaces beneath the player's feet.
Summons Pebbles for assistance.

*After victory:*
The way is clear. Sven walks into the light. Squints.
*Third frequency accepted.*

The diary stays with Sven. But one line remains in the Sphere's memory:
*"Four elements — four voices of one song.*
*Whoever hears all four — can speak to the world in its own language."*

## Quest 4 — The Voice of Wind *(Guardian: Aeros)*

### Trigger

**The Nameless Stranger** in the tavern — always in the corner, always silent.
Today for the first time he looks directly across. He gestures you over.

*"Three Guardians. Not bad."*

The Sphere doesn't understand how he knows.
The body barely spoke to him.

*"The fourth is different. He already knows about you.*
*Sees through people.*
*Go to him yourself. Let him speak first.*
*It's the only way."*

Pause. He returns to his mug. The conversation is over.

*[Mira quietly, if asked]:*
*"The Stranger? He's been here a year. No one knows where from.
Never asks for anything. Speaks rarely — but always precisely.
Bert is afraid of him. I don't know why."*

### The Wind Peaks

Constant wind. Narrow paths over a cliff edge.
Gusties are nearly invisible — they push you off the edge.
Whistlers attack from range, dodge slow strikes.

The top of the main cliff. An open platform.
**Aeros** is already there. A silhouette of swirling air. Glowing eyes.

He doesn't move as the Sphere approaches.
A long pause.
Then — he attacks.

The fastest battle of the four.
Three clones from the air — one real among them.
Make a mistake — you're blown off the cliff.

### After Victory

The wind falls still. Aeros dissolves slowly — reluctantly.
*Fourth frequency accepted.*

*Four voices. They have become one.*
*Something inside the Sphere is whole now. Ready.*
*A rift can be sealed. There. At home.*

## Chapter 1 Finale

### The Celebration

Bert told everyone. The village gathered at the tavern.
Innkeeper Paul rolled out the best barrel. Lights, noise, laughter.

Aldric approaches in silence — holds out a weapon with his personal mark.
*"Good work. For a good fighter."*
No more words needed.

Mira nearby. Looking at something she can't name.
*"You've changed. Not worse — just... different. That's good."*

### The Messenger

Amid the noise and firelight — **a messenger**. Dusty. Rode his horse to exhaustion.
From Valdmar — a neighboring city, two days away.

*"Hunters haven't returned from the forest for a week now.*
*Livestock disappears at night — without a trace.*
*People are acting strange.*
*They speak in words that aren't theirs. They stare at one point."*

Silence.

Bert, quietly, without accusation:
*"It started three weeks ago. Around the time you arrived in Ashworth."*

**The Nameless Stranger** stands up. Pulls on his cloak.
Leaves without a word.
His mug is still warm.

### The Journey Home

*Four frequencies. One pattern.*
*Somewhere in the Steam World a rift is opening right now.*
*The Sphere feels it — like a pull, like a call.*
*The Machine is calling.*

*[Screen fades to black.]*
