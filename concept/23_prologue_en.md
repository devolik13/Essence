# 23. Prologue — "The First"

---

## ACT 1 — NARRATIVE (Steam World)

### Warp Rifts

*[On-screen text / voiceover]*

Late 19th century. The age of steam and electricity.

They began appearing without warning.
Warp rifts — tears in space that open for seconds and vanish.
At the market. In a house. In the middle of the street.
From them emerge creatures — translucent, almost non-material.
They seize people and drag them into the rift.
The rift closes. The person is gone. As if they never existed.

Bullets pass through.
Explosions scatter.
Electric discharge leaves no trace.
Armies are powerless.

No one knows where they come from. No one knows what to do.

---

### The Discovery

*[Laboratory scene — cutscene]*

Nikola Tesla and Marie Curie work with wave measurements in zones
where the rifts appear. Standard instruments show nothing.
Tesla builds an unconventional resonator.
And finds something — a frequency that should not exist.
Not electromagnetic. Not acoustic. Something fundamentally different.
He calls it *"the null frequency"*.

Curie creates a counter-wave. At the point of the experiment
reality begins to behave strangely — as if two spaces briefly touched.

Tesla: *"Somewhere there is another place. It vibrates at the same
frequency as they do."*

Physical matter cannot be transferred — flesh won't pass through.
But brain waves resonate perfectly.

Conclusion: *consciousness can be transferred.*

---

### The First Volunteer

*[Conversation — cutscene]*

A colleague of the scientists — a young assistant — has been listening
to their arguments for three nights running.
Tesla insists: more time is needed, the risk is too great.
Curie says quietly: yesterday a rift opened two blocks from the laboratory.

The assistant sets down his papers and says:

*"I'll go first."*

---

*[Monologue before the machine — on-screen text]*

> *The Transfer Machine looks like a copper-and-glass coffin.*
> *Inside — grilles, wires, the smell of ozone.*
> *Tesla looks at me and says nothing.*
> *Curie says: "We don't know what's there. We don't know how long.*
> *We don't know if you'll come back as yourself."*
>
> *I lie down.*
> *The lid closes.*
> *Darkness.*

---

## ACT 2 — PLAYABLE (Fantasy World)

### Awakening in Another World

*[Player appears as a Sphere — a glowing orb with no body]*

No hands. No weight. Only light and the sensation of vast space.

Below — a village. Living people. The smell of smoke and bread.
Near the resurrection stone stand bodies — as if waiting.

*[Hint: Choose a body to enter this world]*

The player chooses a starting body — warrior, mage, or archer — and inhabits it.

---

### First Steps

*[Text upon taking the first body]*

> *Strange sensation. The body is foreign — but it obeys.*
> *I remember who I am. I remember the laboratory.*
> *But the hands grip the sword hilt as if they always knew how.*
> *This world... it's real.*

The player explores the starting village, completes quests, learns to fight.
With each battle — new skills. With each skill — the understanding that
the physics here is different. What they call "magic" feels like working
with frequencies — the same ones Tesla measured in the laboratory.

---

### The Pull

*[During quest 3 or 4 — mid-task]*

The world lurches.

Sounds have gone muffled. The body feels distant — like looking through
frosted glass. Something pulls backward with growing force.

*No — not yet — I'm not finished —*

*[Screen fades to black.]*

---

## ACT 3 — PLAYABLE (Steam World — the laboratory)

### Waking Up

*[Text]*

> *I open my eyes.*
> *The machine's lid is torn off. Wires burn.*
> *Tesla is shouting something — can't make out the words.*
> *In the doorway stands a creature.*

---

### First Battle in the Real World

*[Playable — combat with a Void creature]*

Instinct. The player uses what they learned in the Fantasy World.
The skill fires. The creature reacts. It's vulnerable.

*[Text during combat]*

> *It sees me. It feels me.*
> *So it works.*

---

### After the Battle

*[Dialogue with Tesla and Curie]*

Curie: *"You used... what was that?"*

Player: *"I don't know. It just worked."*

Tesla, quietly: *"So the resonance is bidirectional.*
*What you learned there — works here.*
*Against them."*

Pause.

Tesla: *"We need more people."*

---

## PROLOGUE FINALE

*[On-screen text]*

> *The next day Tesla rewired the machine.*
> *A week later they built a second one.*
> *A month later — a fifth.*
>
> *The program was named "Essence".*
> *The principle is simple:*
> *you go there, you learn, you come back.*
> *Everyone who comes back becomes a weapon.*
>
> *You were the first.*
> *Now there are many of you.*
>
> **Welcome back.**
